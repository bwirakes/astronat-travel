### 1b. Why the house flip matters

v1 said the trip was about *"communication, siblings, short trips"* —
a light 3rd-house reading. v2 says *"home, roots, family, inner ground"*
— a deep 4th-house reading. For a 30-day stay in Dubai, those are very
different stories. The correct reading aligns with how geodetic-astrology
practitioners (including the PDF) frame Dubai in Pisces/4th-house
territory for someone relocating from Jakarta.

### 1c. Why `activeAngleLines` went from 1 to 0

The v1 hit was *"Mercury on DSC, orb 1.81°"* — that was Brandon's natal
Mercury at 158.88° measured against **Dubai's geodetic DSC** at 157.08°.
Wrong reference angle.

When measured against the **correct relocated DSC** (153.45°), Brandon's
natal Mercury sits 5.44° away — just outside the 5° orb cutoff. So the
v2 reading honestly reports no natal planet within 5° of any of the four
relocated angles at Dubai. That is an *accurate* null result, not a
regression.

Per PDF p.3, natal planets on angles are the most important signatures.
A clean "no contacts" result means Dubai is not an angular city for
Brandon — useful information, not missing information.
